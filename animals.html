Animals!!!

Animals are categorized into two

  1. Domestic animals
  2. Wild animals

Domestic Animals

These are animals that have been tamed and kept by humans as a work animals,food source,or pet.They include:

  • Donkey
  • sheep
  • Dogs
  • Goat

Now,let's take a look at some domesticated

  1. Dogs

    Domestic dogs are mostly kept as pets, though many breeds are capable of surviving on their own, whether it's on a forest or on city streets.

  2. Cats

    Domestic cats are characterised by supple low-slung bodies, finely molded heads, long tails that aid in balanced, and specialized teeth and claws that adapt them admirably to a life of active hunting.

  3. Sheeps

    Sheeps or domestic sheeps (Ovis aries) are domesticated,ruminant mammals typically kept as livestock.
